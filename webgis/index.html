About Cravingjogs

WebGIS known as an informative websites that contain spatial information inside. This technology can be used to collaborate with any information, including culinary destinations. Cravingjogs has a concept as a WebGIS that contains about Yogyakarta’s culinary destinations especially for UMKM with the simple and interactive spatial information in it. You can add your favorite culinary destinations to our database with the spatial information such a location, pictures and etc!
